Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

Come to this eclectic and free film festival under the Brooklyn Bridge Lights.

Spend four steamy summer nights watching films from a variety of genres.

Director: James Cameron

Director: Richard Curtis

A 2003 Christmas Love Story featuring 10 seperate stories which each convey different aspects of love. All of the stories come together in the end to make a great Christmas romance, perfect for getting in the Christmas spirit at any time of the year. More Info

Doctor Zhivago

Actors: Geraldine Chaplin, Omar Sharif

Director: David Lean

Yuri Zhivago is a Russian doctor in the wake of WW1. He falls in love with Tonya, but eventually finds true love with Lara who works with him as a nurse during WW1. Doctor Zhivago's life is compicated by the troublesome political situations associated with the rise fo the Bolsheviks. More Info

Director: Zack Snyder

Director: Orson Welles

Kane's career in the publishing world is intially inspired by doing social justice. However, gradually Kane becomes corrupted by power and goes down a dark path leading to an attempted suicide by his wife. Kane's story shows how greed can corrupt innocent souls. More Info

Director: Nick Cassevetes

An elderly man in a nursing home named Duke retells a love story from his notebook to another occupant. The story Duke tells revolves around the enduring power of love to break through societal social class barriers since the two lovers occupy different social classes. More Info

Indiana Jones travels to Egypt in search of the Ark of the Covenant. He encounters old friends, finds new romance, and engages with Nazi's, snakes and evil curses. The ark of the Covenant's secrets are released in a thrillingly mystical showdown with the Nazi's! More Info

Fun Facts About the Titanic

  1. Titanic is the second highest grossing film ever.
  2. At the time of filming Titanic was the most expensive movie ever filmed.
  3. Gwyneth Paltrow and Matthew McConaughey almost got the roles of Rose and Jack over Kate Winslet and Leonardo Di Caprio.
  4. There was a real Jack Dawson who died on the Titanic.
  5. The cast and crew were drugged when their food was spiked with PCP.
  6. The Titanic was initially expected to be a flop due to how expensive the film was to make.

Titanic Summary

Cast and Crew

Actors/Actresses: Leonardo Di Caprio, Kate Winslet, Bill Paxton, Cathy Bates, Billy Zane.

Director: James Cameron

Producers: James Cameron, and John Landau

Screen Writer: James Cameron

Production Company: Paramount Pictures, 21st Century Fox, and Lightstorm Entertainment.

Plot

In 1996 treasure Hunter Robert Brock and his team of scientists scavenge the wreck of the Titanic in search of lost treasures left behind by the Titanic's wealthy occupants. Rose, a former passenger of the Titanic is brought aboard the research vessel where she retells her story aboard the Titanic, and her Romantic endeavours with Jack Dawson. In her story Rose is suppost to marry a wealth man for his wealth, but instead falls in love with a penniless artist, Jack Dawson, while travelling on the Titanic's maiden voyage. Rose's fiance and her mother disapprove of Jack, but Rose ends up leaving her family to be with Jack as the Titanic sinks after hitting an ice berg.

About

Welcome to the Brooklyn Bridge Film Festival. This years film festival will feature a wide variety of celebrated films from Halloween to The Notebook. The festival will thus feature something to suit everyones mood from Romantic and light hearted films to epics of biblical proportions.

The film festival will take place directly beside the Brooklyn Bridge, and you will have the privledge of watching the lights of Manhattan as a backdrop for the films being screened. Large sound systems will be set up to blare the movies all over Brooklyn. Also, food will be available from local vendors.

The Brooklyn Film festival serves as a great place to meet film buffs to discuss your passion for film. We encourage everyone to take advantage of this absolutely free film festival. We just ask you register using the registration form on this website to give us an idea of the amount of people to expect.
